Performs one of the batch matrix-\/matrix operations on each group of matrices

\[ C[i] = \alpha[i] \times A[i] \times B[i] + \beta[i] \times C[i] \] or \[ C[i] = \alpha[i] \times B[i] \times A[i] + \beta[i] \times C[i] \]
